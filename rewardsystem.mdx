## **Ice Staking Reward System**

When staking with a **validator or Liquid Staking Token (LST) on Solana**, you earn rewards from multiple sources.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="chart-line" title="Four Major Reward Streams">
    1. **Native Yield / Inflation Rewards** – Earned from Solana’s staking inflation mechanism.
    2. **MEV Rewards** – Captured through **Jito tips**, optimizing extra yield from network transactions.
    3. **Block Rewards** – Additional incentives for validators securing the network.
    4. **Exclusive Staker Rewards** – The **top 1% of stakers** receive extra rewards **in USDC.**
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="clock" title="Automatic Epoch-Based Payouts">
    Rewards are distributed **automatically** at the end of each **epoch (~2-3 days)** based on the **commission set by the validator operator.**
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
